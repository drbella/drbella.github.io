Short Stories

  
 


A Room of One’s Own magazine is a collection of short stories, poems, reviews, and commentary by, for, and about women. Produced since 1975 by a volunteer editorial collective, Room is a forum in which women can share their unique perspective on the world, each other and themselves. In 2005, Bella's first short story, Scarecrow, was published in Room.

Ellwood, B. (2005). Scarecrow. A Room of One's Own: Celebrating 25 years of the best in women's literature and criticism, 28:2, 67-92, Vancouver: Growing Room Collective.

Ellwood-Clayton, B. (2009). Melbourne la. A Untitled, Issue 2, 1-13, Melbourne: Busybird Publishing and Design.
